## 3. Business Recommendations

### 3.1 Conversion-Focused Growth Strategy

* Shift strategic focus from **new pump additions to installed base conversion**
* Prioritize activation of inactive and standby pumps
* Introduce conversion KPIs at regional and service-person levels

---

### 3.2 Service Coverage Optimization

* Strengthen service-person engagement in regions with high inactive potential
* Use service-level performance data for targeted coaching and accountability
* Align service coverage with installed base density

---

### 3.3 Customer Retention & Risk Mitigation

* Identify high-value customers with declining activity
* Initiate proactive engagement plans to prevent pump loss
* Track customer-level conversion ratios over time

---

### 3.4 Market Share Stabilization

* Monitor market share trends quarterly instead of annually
* Correlate order booking performance with operational potential
* Use dashboard insights as a standard input for leadership reviews

---
